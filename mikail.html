Mika'il Abdul-Karim, BMS

As an international personal trainer, student of biomechanics with experience in massage therapy, Mika'il provides a different level of personal fitness to his clients. With clients in Thailand, the Philippines, Qatar, and Dubai.

Mika'il’s been body building since 2000 studying under the tutelage of Michael Thaire, owner and fitness coach of Body World Gym in Philadelphia.

He’s continued his education earning certifications in radiofrequency, cavitation, slimming therapy, and cool sculpturing from Florida Medical College with nutritional expertise gained through study with Dr. Paul Brown Bodhise and The Art of Wellness and Nutrition and earning his biomechanics certification from National Exercise & Sports Trainers Association (NESTA).
