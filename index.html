Mika'il Abdul-Karim, BMS

As an international personal trainer, student of biomechanics with experience in massage therapy, Mika'il provides a different level of personal fitness to his clients. With clients in Thailand, the Philipines, Qatar, and Dubai, Mika'il is in demand and helps clients with a variety of different fitness goals. Diverse Body Sculpting offers flexible schedules of 30-minute personal training sessions, meal plans and fitness education to help clients reach their personal best. Using hair samples to research DNA helps assure that the appropriate food groups and diets are selected for all clients. Natural remedies and homeopathic formulas are also used to help with one’s overall heath.

Mika'il’s been body building since 2000 studying under the tutelage of Michael Thaire, owner and fitness coach of Body World Gym in Philadelphia. He’s continued his education earning certifications in radiofrequency, cavitation, slimming therapy, and cool sculpturing from Florida Medical College with nutritional expertise gained through study with Dr. Paul Brown Bodhise and The Art of Wellness and Nutrition and earning his biomechanics certification from National Exercise & Sports Trainers Association (NESTA).
